FAQ

Common questions from property teams.

How often can service be scheduled?

Service frequency can be tailored based on property type, resident volume, and operational needs.

Do you only serve apartment communities?

No. KoreValet can also support HOAs, townhome associations, student housing, senior living, and extended stay properties.

Can you help with dog waste stations?

Yes. KoreValet can provide dog waste station trash removal, bag refills, and replacement supplies as needed.

Can services be customized by property?

Yes. We can help build a property-specific service plan based on layout, resident needs, shared spaces, and collection goals.

Do you offer quotes and consultations?

Yes. Use the inquiry form to request a custom quote or book a consultation for your property.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

It helps to include location, unit count, approximate number of buildings, current provider or setup, and preferred start timeline.

Can KoreValet support student housing or senior living?

Yes. We can tailor service plans for student housing, senior living, extended stay, HOAs, townhome communities, and traditional apartment properties.

Can you provide both valet trash and pet station support?

Yes. Many communities combine valet trash service with dog waste station trash removal, bag refills, and supply replacement.

Still have questions about your property?

Tell us about your community and we can help outline the best service structure for your layout, resident needs, and operational goals.
