Common questions

The basics, answered clearly.

What does KoreValet provide?

KoreValet provides valet trash for apartment communities, plus recycling support and dog waste station service as needed.

What property types do you serve?

We focus on apartment communities and also support HOAs, townhome associations, student housing, senior living, and extended stay properties.

How does the service work?

Residents place secured bags in the designated area before the scheduled window, and our team completes the collection based on your approved service plan.

Do you offer dog waste station service?

Yes. We can help with trash removal, bag refills, and dispenser replacements for pet areas and dog waste stations.

Can you help us estimate ROI?

Yes. The homepage includes an ROI calculator that gives a quick estimate of revenue, cost, profit, and return based on unit count and pricing.

How do we request service?

Use the contact form on the homepage or email management@korevalet.com with your property details and we’ll follow up.
